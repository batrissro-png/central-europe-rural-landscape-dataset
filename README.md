# Central Europe Rural Landscape Dataset

A high-resolution drone and terrestrial imagery dataset of rural landscapes in Central Europe, designed for AI/ML training, computer vision, and geospatial analysis.

This repository serves as a **technical overview and entry point** to the dataset.  
The full dataset, documentation, and licensing are hosted on Hugging Face.

## Dataset Overview

- **945 curated images** of rural and natural landscapes
- **Central Europe**, multiple locations and seasons (including winter)
- **Image-based scene understanding**, not object detection
- Combination of **UAV (drone)** and **terrestrial photography**
- Designed for:
  - landscape-level scene classification
  - environmental and land-use analysis
  - geospatial and computer vision research
  - commercial AI model development

Each image is assigned **one primary landscape class** and may include **supplementary tags** describing additional visually significant elements.

## Annotations & Methodology

- Exactly **one primary class per image**
- Optional supplementary tags
- Visually driven annotation philosophy reflecting real-world ambiguity
- No object-level bounding boxes or segmentation masks
